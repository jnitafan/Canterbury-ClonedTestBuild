Pool Access

During the colder months in Melbourne (1st June – 1st October) swimming in our outdoor pool is less favourable and demand from our guests is very low. In the interest of being  environmentally considerate we do not provide heating
during this time.

Guests still wishing to enjoy swimming activities can collect
complimentary passes from reception to Nunawading Aqualink, (a 50m heated multi purpose swimming facility), which is only a 5 minute walk from our Hotel.
